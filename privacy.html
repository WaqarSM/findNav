FindNav Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

FindNav is a browser extension that helps you find and activate text, links, and controls on the page you are viewing.

Data collection

FindNav does not collect any data. Nothing at all is gathered, stored, transmitted, or shared.

  • No browsing history
  • No page content
  • No search queries
  • No personal information
  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No advertising identifiers

How FindNav works

All search and navigation happens locally in your browser, on the current page. FindNav does not send page content or search terms to any server, and it does not include a network backend for product use.

Permissions

FindNav may request permissions needed to run its finder overlay on pages you visit and to open from the toolbar popup. Those permissions are used only to provide FindNav’s on-page features. They are not used to collect or export your data.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be raised in the FindNav GitHub repository.
